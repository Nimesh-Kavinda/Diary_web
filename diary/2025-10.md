## 2025-10-01

- Dependency and architecture day. Updated package configurations. Refactored **BookDetails** with improved data fetching and dynamic routing. Added URL encoding to the CategoryBadge routing so category names with spaces and special characters work correctly in URL paths. Replaced manual pagination in the ViewAll component with **useInfiniteQuery** for proper infinite scroll. Updated ActionButtons and ChapterList for consistent subscription UI, including a mobile-friendly alert style that links users to the rate plans page.
